Overview

TwistNext is a powerful Nextflow pipeline for analyzing Twist NGS Methylation data. It offers quality control, alignment, methylation calling, differential methylation analysis, and post-processing—all in one place.

Get Started

Highlights

  • READ_PROCESSING: Trim and QC raw reads.
  • BISMARK_ANALYSIS: Bisulfite alignment, deduplication, and methylation extraction.
  • QC_REPORTING: Aggregate reports with MultiQC.
  • DIFFERENTIAL_METHYLATION: Analyze methylation patterns with EdgeR and MethylKit.
  • POST_PROCESSING: Visualize and summarize results.
