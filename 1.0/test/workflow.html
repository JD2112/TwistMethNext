Workflow

TwistNext’s modular design ensures flexibility and adaptability to various research requirements. Below is an overview of the workflow:

Steps

  1. READ_PROCESSING: Quality control with FastQC and trimming with Trim Galore.
  2. BISMARK_ANALYSIS: Alignment, deduplication, sorting, and methylation calling.
  3. QC_REPORTING: Aggregated reports with MultiQC.
  4. DIFFERENTIAL_METHYLATION: Analysis with EdgeR and MethylKit.
  5. POST_PROCESSING: Visualization and statistics.
